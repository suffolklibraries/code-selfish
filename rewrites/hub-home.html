MakerSpace

Situated in the Lab, our MakerSpace is an open, friendly public space to design and create.The MakerSpace offers a range of equipment including two 3D printers, a 3D scanner, two computer coding kits and two professional sewing machines. Find out about our facilities in the MakerSpace.

Are you experienced with fashion and textile design, coding or 3D printers? We’re looking for skilled and enthusiastic volunteers to help support our newly built MakerSpace. Contact us at hub@suffolklibraries.co.uk or call 01473 263827 to get involved!
