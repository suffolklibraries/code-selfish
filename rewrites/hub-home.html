Code club

Code Club is a nationwide network of free volunteer-led coding clubs for children. Children learn while using their imagination in creative projects. Accompanied children aged between 6-14 can take part.

Bring your own laptop or use one of our library Chromebooks. Tablets are not suitable for this course. Contact the hub at hub@suffolklibraries.co.uk

Business advice

We have two New Anglia Growth Hub advisors working on a fortnightly, rotating basis. They advise on topics including exporting, digital support, intellectual property, finance, grants and vouchers, and starting up a business.

MakerSpace

Situated in the Lab, our MakerSpace is an open, friendly public space to design and create. The MakerSpace offers a range of equipment including two 3D printers, a 3D scanner, two computer coding kits and two professional sewing machines. Find out about our facilities in the MakerSpace.

Are you experienced with fashion and textile design, coding or 3D printers? We’re looking for skilled and enthusiastic volunteers to help support our newly built MakerSpace. Contact us at hub@suffolklibraries.co.uk or call 01473 263827 to get involved!
